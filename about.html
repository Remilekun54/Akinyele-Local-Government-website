Welcome to Akinyele Local Government

Est 1976

Our Story

Welcome to the official website of Akinyele Local Government, the heart of progress and community in Ibadan, Oyo State. Akinyele is more than just a place on the map; it is a vibrant hub of culture, enterprise, and opportunity. Established in 1976, Akinyele was carved out of the former Ibadan Municipal Government and has since grown into a metropolitan area where tradition and modernity coexist harmoniously.

Our Vision is to be the most prosperous and progressive local government in Oyo State, driven by a commitment to good governance, sustainable development, and community well-being.

Who We Are

Our local government is a melting pot, home to a diverse population of citizens from different tribes and nationalities. This diversity is our strength, fostering a community known for its friendly and accommodating nature. We are proud of our rich Yoruba culture and the historical sites that dot our landscape, such as the Bowen's Tomb and Ope Ijaye, which stand as monuments to our enduring heritage.

Under the current leadership of our elected Chairman, Hon. Wole Akinleye, our administration is committed to building a brighter future. We are focused on implementing projects that drive economic growth, improve public services, and create opportunities for all our residents. Our vision is to transform Akinyele into the richest local government in Oyo State, a goal that is rapidly becoming a reality through our strategic initiatives and partnerships.

Our Vision

Our vision is to build a united, prosperous, and sustainable community where diversity is celebrated as strength. We aim to foster inclusiveness, preserve our Yoruba heritage, and transform our local government into a hub of culture, innovation, and economic opportunity for every resident.

Our Mission

Our mission is to serve the people with integrity and dedication by implementing projects that drive growth, improve public services, and create opportunities for all. We are committed to promoting unity, preserving our cultural heritage, and ensuring every resident enjoys a better quality of life.

About Us: Our History & Demographics

Akinyele Local Government was officially created in 1976 and is named after the late Olubadan, Isaac Babalola Akinyele. Our headquarters are situated in Moniya, serving as the administrative nerve center for the 12 wards that make up our local government area .

The population of Akinyele, last measured at 211,359, is predominantly made up of farmers and artisans. However, the presence of major institutions like the International Institute of Tropical Agriculture (IITA), Nigeria Institute of Social and Economic Research (NISER), and the Federal School of Statistics has attracted a diverse mix of people, making our communities bustling and metropolitan.

Economy: The Engine of Our Growth

The economy of Akinyele Local Government is anchored in agriculture and commerce. Our fertile land falls within the forest and derived savannah zones, making it ideal for cultivating a wide range of crops. Vibrant markets like Ojoo, Moniya, and Sasa are hubs of trade, while collaborations with institutions such as IITA enable improved crop yields and innovation.

The Inland Dry Port, Agro-processing Zone, and Agribusiness Industrial hubs (expected to generate over 100,000 jobs) are reshaping our economic future.

Projects & Achievements

  • FS4A Project: Improving food safety with IITA (2024).
  • Agribusiness Hubs: Hosting 40+ agribusinesses, impacting 500k farmers (2025).
  • Infrastructure: Inland Dry Port & Ilutuntun City development (2023).
  • Roads: Ojo-Moniya dualisation & Ojo-Ayegun reconstruction.
  • Youth Empowerment: Skills, training, entrepreneurship programs.
  • Healthcare: Upgrading Primary Health Centres across wards.
  • Security: Strengthened collaborations with law enforcement.
