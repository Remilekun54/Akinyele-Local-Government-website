Our Story

Welcome to the official website of Akinyele Local Government, the heart of progress and community in Ibadan, Oyo State. Akinyele is more than just a place on the map; it is a vibrant hub of culture, enterprise, and opportunity. Established in 1976, Akinyele was carved out of the former Ibadan Municipal Government and has since grown into a metropolitan area where tradition and modernity coexist harmoniously.

  • We are the power house of change
  • We integrate Artificial Intelligence to Governance
  • We are the pace setter

Our Vision is to be the most prosperous and progressive local government in Oyo State, driven by a commitment to good governance, sustainable development, and community well-being.
