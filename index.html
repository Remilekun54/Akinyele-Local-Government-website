Our Story

Welcome to the official website of Akinyele Local Government, the heart of progress and community in Ibadan, Oyo State. Akinyele is more than just a place on the map; it is a vibrant hub of culture, enterprise, and opportunity. Established in 1976, Akinyele was carved out of the former Ibadan Municipal Government and has since grown into a metropolitan area where tradition and modernity coexist harmoniously.

Our Vision is to be the most prosperous and progressive local government in Oyo State, driven by a commitment to good governance, sustainable development, and community well-being.

Road Infrastructure and Modernization

Our administration is dedicated to improving connectivity and transportation. We have actively engaged in the rehabilitation and reconstruction of critical roads, including the dualisation of the Ojo-Moniya Road and the fixing of the Ojo-Ayegun Road. These projects are essential for easing the movement of people and goods, stimulating local commerce, and attracting investment to our communities.

Support for Local Farmers and Agricultural Programs

Recognizing that agriculture is the backbone of our economy, we have launched programs to support our farmers. This includes providing access to modern farming techniques, improved crop varieties, and strategic partnerships with agricultural research institutions. Our goal is to enhance productivity, reduce post-harvest losses, and ensure food security for all residents.

Ilutuntun City Project

As a forward-thinking administration, we are collaborating on the development of Ilutuntun City, a modern urban hub that will transform the landscape of our local government. This project is designed to attract commercial activities, provide high-quality housing, and create new job opportunities, setting a new standard for urban living in the region.

Youth Empowerment and Skill Acquisition Programs

We believe in investing in our future leaders. Our administration has commissioned various youth empowerment initiatives focused on skill acquisition and entrepreneurship. These programs provide vocational training and business development support, enabling our youth to become self-reliant and contribute to the economic growth of Akinyele.

Public Health and Primary Healthcare Centers

The health and well-being of our citizens are paramount. We are committed to upgrading and equipping our Primary Healthcare Centers across all wards. By improving these facilities and ensuring a steady supply of essential medicines, we are making quality healthcare accessible to every family, especially in rural communities.

Security and Public Safety Initiatives

Our administration has prioritized the safety of our communities. We are collaborating with law enforcement agencies and local community leaders to implement various security measures. These initiatives include providing logistical support to security personnel and fostering community-based security networks to ensure a peaceful and secure environment for our residents and businesses.
